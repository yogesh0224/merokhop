Essential Vaccines for Your Family

Discover key vaccines for child and maternal health, aligned with Nepal's National Immunization Program (2025).

Childhood Vaccines

BCG

Administered at birth to protect against tuberculosis.

Learn More

Pentavalent (DPT-HepB-Hib)

Doses at 6, 10, 14 weeks. Protects against diphtheria, pertussis, tetanus, hepatitis B, Haemophilus influenzae type b.

Learn More

OPV

Oral doses at 6, 10, 14 weeks to prevent polio.

Learn More

PCV

Doses at 6, 10 weeks, booster at 9 months. Protects against pneumococcal diseases.

Learn More

fIPV

Doses at 14 weeks and 9 months to prevent polio.

Learn More

MR

Doses at 9 and 15 months to protect against measles and rubella.

Learn More

JE

Dose at 12 months in endemic areas to protect against Japanese encephalitis.

Learn More

TCV

Dose at 15 months to protect against typhoid fever.

Learn More

HPV

Single dose for girls aged 9-14 years to prevent cervical cancer.

Learn More

Maternal and Additional Vaccines

Td

For pregnant women: 2 doses in first pregnancy, 1 dose in subsequent to protect against tetanus and diphtheria.

Learn More

Tdap

Recommended for pregnant women between 27-36 weeks to protect newborns from whooping cough.

Learn More

Flu

Recommended during pregnancy to prevent severe flu complications for mothers and babies.

Learn More

COVID-19

Safe for pregnant women, reduces severe illness; consult your doctor for timing.

Learn More

BCG Vaccine Details

Administered at birth to protect against tuberculosis. Side effects may include a small scar at injection site. Consult doctor if fever persists.

Pentavalent Vaccine Details

Protects against five diseases. Given in three doses. Common side effects: mild fever, soreness.

OPV Vaccine Details

Oral polio vaccine. Easy administration. Helps eradicate polio in Nepal.

PCV Vaccine Details

Pneumococcal conjugate vaccine. Prevents pneumonia and meningitis.

fIPV Vaccine Details

Fractional inactivated polio vaccine. Booster for polio protection.

MR Vaccine Details

Measles-Rubella vaccine. Two doses for full immunity.

JE Vaccine Details

Japanese Encephalitis vaccine. Crucial in endemic regions.

TCV Vaccine Details

Typhoid conjugate vaccine. Protects against typhoid fever.

HPV Vaccine Details

Human Papillomavirus vaccine. Prevents cervical cancer in girls.

Td Vaccine Details

Tetanus-Diphtheria vaccine for pregnant women.

Tdap for pertussis protection during pregnancy.

New Tdap Guidelines for 2025

Updated Tdap recommendations to enhance newborn protection during pregnancy. The Ministry of Health has mandated administration between 27-36 weeks for optimal antibody transfer.

Postpartum Vaccine Campaign in Nepal

Free MMR vaccines for new mothers and infants in rural areas, extended to December 2025. This initiative aims to reduce measles outbreaks in vulnerable populations.

COVID-19 Booster Recommendations

New boosters advised for pregnant women, available at major hospitals as of October 2025. Studies show reduced risk of severe disease and preterm birth.
